Lamborghini Countach LPI 800-4

The Lamborghini Countach LPI 800-4 is a masterpiece that marries iconic design with cutting-edge innovation. Paying homage to the legendary Countach of the 1970s, this modern marvel features a robust V12 engine combined with a 48-volt electric motor, delivering a breathtaking 802 horsepower. Its striking angular design is enhanced by advanced aerodynamics, while the use of lightweight carbon fiber ensures a perfect balance of power and agility.


Lamborghini Essenza SCV12

The Lamborghini Essenza SCV12 is a hypercar that epitomizes pure performance and cutting-edge technology. Born from Lamborghini's rich racing heritage, this track-exclusive marvel boasts a naturally aspirated V12 engine, delivering over 830 horsepower. Crafted with an ultra-lightweight carbon fiber monocoque chassis, the Essenza SCV12 achieves unparalleled power-to-weight ratio and agility. Its aerodynamics are inspired by GT3 racing, offering maximum downforce and precision handling.
